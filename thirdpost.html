How is healthcare biased

Posted by by Xinyi Jin on April 28, 2023

What are the main areas of gender bias in the medical field?

For example, certain medical conditions may be overlooked or misdiagnosed in women due to biases about gender norms and expectations. For example, women may be more likely to have their symptoms ignored or attributed to psychological or emotional factors.

Women may be less likely to receive certain medical treatments or interventions due to biases around gender roles and stereotypes. For example, women may be prescribed fewer pain medications than men, or receive less aggressive treatment in some cases.

Women are often underrepresented in medical research, which may lead to a lack of understanding of how medical conditions and treatments affect women differently than men. This can lead to inadequate treatment or care for women.

Certain medical conditions, such as mental health disorders or reproductive health issues, can be stigmatized or marginalized, leading to inadequate or biased care for those who experience these issues.

Commentary on The Healer · by Bynum, William E. MD
